Infant Care

6 weeks - 12 months

Program Overview

Our infant care program provides a nurturing, safe environment where your baby can grow and develop. We focus on building trust and security through responsive caregiving.

What We Offer

  • Low teacher-to-child ratios for personalized attention
  • Age-appropriate sensory activities
  • Daily communication about feeding, diapering, and naps
  • Safe, clean environment designed for infants
  • Tummy time and motor skill development
