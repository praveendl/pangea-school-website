The Meaning of Pangea

Pangea comes from the Greek word "pangaia" translating to "all of the Earth". This definition inspired the idea of basing our logo around connection and community.

The icon at first looks like a globe with a jumble of continents together, but taking a closer look reveals a mother elephant nurturing a baby elephant. The elephants were chosen as they resemble the shape of Africa, Pangea as a whole, and are also symbols of connection, trust, and family in many African tales.

Just like the nurturing bond between mother and baby elephant, we create a community where every child is supported, connected, and loved.
